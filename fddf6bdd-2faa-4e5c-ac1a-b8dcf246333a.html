Description

  • HOME SYSTEM
    Lonely Island is provided as your private place. You can explore it, collect different building resources to improve your environment and buildings, attract businessman and open more advanced functions.
  • THE WORLD
    Our story happened in a land called Ground Hong with over 30 scenery including villages, towns, cities, islands, graves, ruins and mountains. Besides the main task, our branchy ones also support you have a wide knowledge of this world.
  • BATTLE MODE
    Our TBS system allows any arms, skills, buff and backup with 5V5 mode. Your speed decides your order, and you have to give a best tactics combination.
  • TAME THE MONSTERS
    Most of the monsters in the game can be tamed, and tamed monsters can be used as the guard to add attributes for the role. The guard will also have a chance to fight and unleash skills at the same time as the role is attacking. Tame the monster need to buy special props, "Tame Token" and use it in battle to wounded monster;
  • ATTRIBUTES
    We provide many rare attributes for you. Use them properly will help you a lot.
